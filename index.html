About Me

Jon Bjork

I've been playing guitar since 1992 and studied at the Guitar Institute of Technology in Los Angeles, learning from musicians like Brett Garsed and T.J. Helmerich.

I started teaching full-time in 2001 and have worked with students at every level ever since. I've also spent years composing orchestral music, nearly 700 tracks for Epidemic Sound, heard on channels like MrBeast and Hot Ones.

My goal is simple: make the path to better playing as clear and direct as possible. Whether that's through coaching, courses, or free lessons — I want every guitarist I work with to actually feel the progress.
